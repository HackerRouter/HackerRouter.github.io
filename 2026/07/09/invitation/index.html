Invitation

Yes. This time, it is not some embarrassing stream-of-consciousness piece, but more of a random note, maybe.

In case you do not know me - though honestly, who tf is even reading this - I… okay, wait, who am I?

NOT_HR, of course.

No, no, no. It should be NOT NOT_HR, since I used another name for the game.

I got used to playing Marvel Rivals last year and gained, well, fame and friends, of course, in another gaming community.

Recently, the game is about to receive a huge update. It is not just going to change the balance; the changes are so massive that it may feel like a completely new game. So it is probably a nice time for new players to give it a try, and for players who quit to come back.

So, uh, I received quite a few invitations asking me to return to that stupid game again.

So I thought and thought, and came up with the following reasons:

Reasons why I should return:

  • I am good at the game, so I can gain that dopamine hit.

  • I am good at every hero, which means I probably will not be at a disadvantage in the new version.

  • I have a lot of friends who are willing to drag me back in.

  • Marvel Rivals is still a fun and satisfying game.

  • I am curious about what has happened in the game and the community while I was gone.

Reasons why I should not return:

  • I am FUCKING studying.

  • My GPA.

  • My time is limited.

  • Competitive games are meaningless.

  • I should not invest even more sunk cost into it.

  • I really want a new update suppressor of the other stupid block game uwu

Time. Yeah, time.

Time is a very fair thing.

Wherever you invest most of it, it gives you the return you deserve.

As a result, I think I will just keep studying instead.

AND for that other stupid block game, putting it on hold is obviously the only choice.
