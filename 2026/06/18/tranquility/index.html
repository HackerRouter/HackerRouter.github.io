Tranquility

Stare, simply stare. A letter on my screen. I stare at it.

What is its structure? What color is it? What shape is it? What does it represent? What word does it form?

I do not know. I just stare.

There is no deadline. No competition. No concern for the future. No anxiety about myself.

I just stare. I just stare at the letter.

There are no goals to pursue, no tasks to complete, no code to dig through, no project to finish.

Nothing demands anything of me.

I stare.

I just stare.

It feels so great. And I do not even need to know that it feels great.

Never in my life have I felt such tranquility.

I was uncertain about everything.

My mind was full of thoughts.

I have always been searching for meaning in everything.

I could not stop. It would never stop. It just could not stop.

Until today.

Because of Atomoxetine? A placebo effect?

No. No.

I do not need to know.

I just stare.
