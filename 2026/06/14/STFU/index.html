STFU

One day, I was staring at the mirror.
I looked at the shell I so desperately wanted to shed.
Then, someone appeared.
“Oh, forever unchangeable, forever truly unaccepted.”
“Shut up.”
I yelled at that person.

One day, I was cleaning my room.
I looked at the memory-laden things about to be thrown away.
Then, someone appeared.
“Oh, time never returns, and all good things must pass.”
“Shut up.”
I yelled at that person.

One day, I was walking on my way home.
I looked at the scenery I couldn’t be more familiar with.
Then, someone appeared.
“Oh, things remain but people change, who remembers you anyway?”
“Shut up.”
I yelled at that person.

One day, I was scrolling through videos.
Then, someone appeared.
“Oh, still wondering why you haven’t gotten the credit you deserve?”
“Shut up.”
I yelled at that person.

One day, I was playing Tetrio.
I looked at the timer in the bottom left corner of the screen.
Then, someone appeared.
“Oh, do you really think you can break your record this time?”
“Shut up.”
I yelled at that person.

One day, I was reading Minecraft code.
I looked at-
Then, someone appeared.
“Oh, do you really think you can find something new and different?”
“Shut up.”
I yelled at that person.

“Can’t you just SHUT THE FUCK UP?
You’ve been judging my every move, day in and day out for decades.
I was supposed to be an optimistic and positive person,
Yet all you do is mock and sneer.”

I screamed out loud.

That person paused for a moment, replied with a smile,

“You extracted all your negative thoughts,
You stripped away the system of how others judge you,
And personified them into me.
Isn’t this exactly what you wanted?”
